About StockerPro

StockerPro is an AI-powered stock analysis tool. Enter 1–3 US stock ticker symbols and it fetches the 3 most recent trading days of historical price data, then sends that data to Google Gemini to generate a plain-English report with a "BUY", "HOLD", or "SELL" recommendation for each ticker.

It is intended as a quick starting point for research — not a trading platform or financial advisory service.

Data Source — massive.com

Historical market data is retrieved from the massive.com Aggregates Bars (OHLC) API. Each request queries a 10-calendar-day lookback window to reliably capture 3 trading days, accounting for weekends and US market holidays. Attributes taken from API for analusis are:

Field Description
Date t Unix timestamp of the trading day (converted to readable date on screen)
Open o Opening price of the symbol on the given day
High h Highest traded price of the symbol on the given day
Low l Lowest traded price of the symbol on the given day
Close c Closing price of the symbol on the given day
Volume v Trading volume of the symbol on the given day
Weighted Avg Volume vw Volume-weighted average price
Number of transactions n Number of transactions of the symbol on the given day

Data returned on the free tier may be delayed and does not reflect real-time prices.

AI Analysis — Google Gemini

Once stock data is fetched, the entire dataset (all tickers, all 3 days) is passed to Google Gemini in a single prompt. The model is instructed to act as a trading analyst and produce a report of no more than 200 words, describing each stock's recent performance and recommending whether to buy, hold, or sell.

Detail Value
Model gemini-2.5-flash (subject to change)
Library @google/genai
Input 3-day aggregate price data for all requested tickers
Output Plain-text report and summary tables for each ticker displayed on the report page
Max output length ~200 words (instructed in prompt)

The AI has no access to news, company fundamentals, earnings data, or broader market context beyond the 3-day price history provided.

Limitations

Limitation Details
Massive free tier rate limit 5 API calls per minute. This is why StockerPro caps input at 3 tickers per request — each ticker is one call.
Gemini free tier rate limit The Gemini free tier throttles concurrent or high-frequency requests. If the analysis step fails or is slow, this is the likely cause.
3-day data window only Analysis is based solely on 3 trading days of closing price and volume. No weekly, monthly, or fundamental data is considered.
Delayed market data Massive's free tier provides delayed quotes, not real-time prices. Data shown may lag the live market.
US tickers only The Massive endpoint used covers US exchange-listed equities. Tickers must be 1–5 uppercase letters (e.g. AAPL, MSFT, TSLA).
Market hours Data updates only on US trading days. Requests made on weekends or holidays will reflect the most recent available session.
